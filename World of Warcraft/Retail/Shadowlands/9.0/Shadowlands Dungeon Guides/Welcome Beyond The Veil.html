Welcome Beyond The Veil

With Shadowlands officially released it is time to take a quick dive into each of the eight brand new dungeons and their boss fights. While not quite as large as a raid guide, each of these smaller guides should provide you with enough knowledge to tackle these fun group activites on mythic difficulty and beyond. I will not be going over the differences between normal heroic and mythic inside of these guides. The reason for this is I actually have only done these on mythic, thus running them on lower levels for guides would be a waste of my time.

With that being said, I hope these written guides are helpful. For any further inquiries feel free to use the email listed in my Nyalotha guide to contact me.
